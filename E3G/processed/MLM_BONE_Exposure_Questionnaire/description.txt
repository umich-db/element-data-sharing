"This dataset is a comprehensive survey on environmental exposures, specifically focusing on the usage and frequency of various personal care and household products. It includes variables such as the use of nail polish, skin lightening products, deodorants, perfumes, soaps, shampoos, and other toiletries, as well as household items like laundry products, dish soap, and pesticides. The dataset also explores dietary habits related to the consumption of canned foods and beverages, and the use of clay dishes for food preparation and storage. Each variable is meticulously described with possible values indicating frequency of use, ranging from daily to never, and includes options for uncertainty or lack of knowledge."
