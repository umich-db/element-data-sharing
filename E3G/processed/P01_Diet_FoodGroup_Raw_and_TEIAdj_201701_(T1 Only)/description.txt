"This dataset provides a comprehensive list of food items categorized into various groups such as dairy, fruits, meats, vegetables, grains, desserts, beverages, and more, each with a corresponding adjusted variable for total energy intake (TEI). Each food item is identified by a unique variable name and is described in both English and Spanish, although some descriptions are missing. The dataset includes both raw and TEI-adjusted data for each food category, allowing for detailed nutritional analysis and dietary assessment. This structured format supports extensive research and analysis in nutritional science, particularly in understanding dietary patterns and their health impacts."
