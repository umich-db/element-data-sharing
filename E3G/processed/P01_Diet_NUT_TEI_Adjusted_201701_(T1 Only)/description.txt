"This dataset comprises a comprehensive collection of nutritional information for various food items, detailing both macronutrients and micronutrients. It includes data on energy content (both in kcal and kJ), macronutrients like proteins, fats, and carbohydrates, and micronutrients such as vitamins and minerals. Additionally, the dataset provides information on food fortification, including the presence of fortified calcium, iron, zinc, vitamin A, and vitamin C, as well as details on food processing levels. Each entry is meticulously cataloged with identifiers and descriptions in multiple languages, ensuring a broad utility for nutritional analysis and dietary assessments."
