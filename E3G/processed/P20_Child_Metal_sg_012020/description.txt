"This dataset comprises various columns representing different elements and their specific properties, each identified by a unique variable name. The elements included are nickel (Ni), copper (Cu), molybdenum (Mo), antimony (Sb), barium (Ba), lead (Pb), iron (Fe), arsenic (As), selenium (Se), aluminum (Al), manganese (Mn), cobalt (Co), zinc (Zn), and cadmium (Cd). Additionally, the dataset includes a column for specific gravity (spec_grav). Each element is associated with its chemical symbol and measured properties, providing a comprehensive overview of their characteristics."
