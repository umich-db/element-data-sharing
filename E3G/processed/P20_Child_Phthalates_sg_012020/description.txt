"This dataset contains information on various environmental phenols and parabens, categorized under two main panels: UEP (Environmental Phenols) and UPB (Parabens). Each entry includes the full panel name, analysis panel, variable, full analyte name, analyte code, and CAS number. The dataset covers a range of compounds such as Benzophenone-3, Bisphenol A, Triclocarban, and various parabens like Butyl Paraben and Methyl Paraben. The data is structured with the first row serving as headers, followed by detailed rows for each analyte, providing a comprehensive overview of these chemical substances."
