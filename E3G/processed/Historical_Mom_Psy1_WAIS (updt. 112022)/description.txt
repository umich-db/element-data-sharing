"This dataset contains a comprehensive collection of variables related to child development, environmental factors, and parental characteristics, structured across multiple stages and assessments. Key variables include project identifiers, folio numbers, stages of data collection, dates, and scores from psychological assessments such as the WAIS test for mothers. Additional data points cover a wide range of environmental exposures, dietary habits, and socio-economic factors that could influence child development. The dataset is designed to facilitate detailed analysis of factors affecting child health and development, with variables meticulously labeled and formatted for clarity and ease of use."
