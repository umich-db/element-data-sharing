"This dataset contains detailed nutritional information for various food items, categorized under multiple columns such as 'folio', 'proyecto', 'fluoruro', and 'fluorurobeb'. Each entry includes data on macronutrients and micronutrients such as proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals, with specific focus on elements like iron, zinc, and phytic acid. The dataset also includes information on the density of liquids, edible portions, and whether the food is cooked, raw, or prepared. Additionally, it covers data on food fortification with essential nutrients like calcium, iron, and vitamins, and the classification of processed and enriched foods, providing a comprehensive resource for nutritional analysis."
