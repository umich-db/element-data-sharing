"The P20 Master Database contains a comprehensive collection of variables from questionnaires, laboratory measures, and historical data related to maternal demographics, lead exposure, and anthropometry. The dataset includes questionnaire data entered in Mexico, lab data on hormones, bisphenol A (BPA), phthalates, and methylation markers, as well as variables on child and maternal health and demographics. Laboratory methods for assays conducted at Michigan are briefly summarized, and the dataset encompasses a wide range of topics such as physical activities, dietary habits, personal care product usage, environmental exposures, and various health indicators measured at different stages of the participants' lives."
