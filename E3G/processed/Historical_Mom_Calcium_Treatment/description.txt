"This dataset contains information on calcium treatment supplements across different cohorts. The columns include 'PROYECTO', 'FOLIO', and 'calcium_trt', with 'calcium_trt' detailing the distribution of calcium supplementation among various groups: no supplement, during pregnancy, and during lactation. The data is organized into cohorts labeled C1, BI, PL, and SF, with corresponding totals and subgroup counts for each type of calcium treatment. The dataset provides a structured overview of calcium supplement usage within the study population."
