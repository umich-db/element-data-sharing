"This dataset comprises a comprehensive collection of nutritional information, categorized into various food groups and detailing both macro and micronutrients. It includes data on protein, fat, carbohydrates, vitamins (such as Vitamin B6, B12, C, E), minerals (like iron, zinc, selenium), and other specific nutrients such as omega fatty acids, caffeine, and cholesterol. Each entry is meticulously sourced, with details on the origin of the data for macronutrients and micronutrients, ensuring the reliability of the information. Additionally, the dataset provides insights into food processing levels and the presence of fortified nutrients, making it a valuable resource for nutritional analysis and dietary planning."
