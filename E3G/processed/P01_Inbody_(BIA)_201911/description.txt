"This dataset contains comprehensive body composition data collected using InBody technology, which provides detailed information on various physical parameters such as total body water, protein content, mineral levels, body fat mass, skeletal muscle mass, and more. Each entry includes a range of measurements, both actual and reference values, such as minimum and maximum thresholds suggested by InBody based on age and gender. Additional data points include basal metabolic rate calculated using the Harris-Benedict formula, fitness scores, and personalized recommendations for weight, fat, and muscle control. The dataset is structured to assist in monitoring and managing individual health and fitness goals effectively."
