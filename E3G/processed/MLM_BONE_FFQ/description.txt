"This dataset is part of the National Health and Nutrition Survey 2019 conducted by the National Institute of Public Health in Mexico. It captures the frequency of food consumption among individuals aged 12 and above over the past seven days, detailing the types and quantities of various food items consumed. The dataset includes a wide range of food categories such as dairy products, fruits, vegetables, meats, cereals, and beverages, along with portion sizes and consumption frequency. It also records demographic information like age and project identifiers, providing a comprehensive overview of dietary habits in the surveyed population."
