"This dataset, titled \"R01MB-M_BONE iDXA,\" provides a comprehensive collection of variables related to body composition analysis. It includes detailed measurements of various body mass components such as fat mass, lean mass, bone mass, and total mass, segmented by different body regions (e.g., arms, legs, trunk) and specific areas like android and gynoid regions. The dataset also contains demographic information such as patient ID, birthdate, gender, and visit date. The data is structured to facilitate the analysis of body composition changes across different body parts and over time, making it valuable for research in fields like nutrition, fitness, and medical diagnostics."
