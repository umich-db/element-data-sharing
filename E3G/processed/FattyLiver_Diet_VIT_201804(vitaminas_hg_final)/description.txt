"This dataset provides a comprehensive list of nutritional information for various food items, detailing both macronutrients and micronutrients. It includes data on sources and codes for nutrient information, food group classifications, and specific nutrient content per 100g such as proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals. Additionally, the dataset covers information on food fortification with essential nutrients like calcium, iron, and vitamins, as well as the classification of processed foods. This data is crucial for nutritional analysis and can aid in dietary planning and health assessments."
