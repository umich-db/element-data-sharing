"This dataset contains a comprehensive collection of variables captured through a detailed questionnaire, focusing on various aspects of respondents' demographic, health, and lifestyle characteristics. The data includes information on interview timings, personal and household details, health-related questions, and environmental factors. Each variable, such as 'hora_ini' (start hour), 'edad' (age), and 's2' (gender), is meticulously described with type, size, and potential values, ensuring clarity for analysis. The dataset is structured to facilitate in-depth research on the socio-economic and health conditions of the surveyed population, providing insights through both direct questions and derived variables."
