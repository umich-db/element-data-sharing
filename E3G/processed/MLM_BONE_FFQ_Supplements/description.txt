"This dataset, titled \"MANUAL DE VARIABLES,\" is part of the R01 - MLM-BONE project and focuses on FFQ-Suplementos, which are dietary supplements. It includes a wide range of variables such as 'proyecto', 'folio', 'etapa', 'gpoedad', and 'edad', along with numerous supplement names like 'acido_folico_ss', 'centrum', and 'bedoyecta', each with specific descriptions detailing their composition and intended use. The dataset provides detailed information on various supplements, including their formulations, target demographics (e.g., pediatric or prenatal), and specific health benefits, such as supporting bone health, providing essential vitamins and minerals, or aiding in iron absorption. This comprehensive dataset is designed to facilitate the analysis of supplement usage and its impact on health outcomes."
