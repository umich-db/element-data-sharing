"This dataset contains geographical and address-related information. The columns include identifiers like 'folio' and 'foliocc', various stages labeled 'stage' and 'etapa', and a timestamp 'fecha_gps'. Geolocation data is provided with 'latitud_' and 'longitud_'. Address details are comprehensive, covering 'calle_' (street), 'no_' (number), 'colonia_' (neighborhood), 'deleg_' (delegation), 'municipio' (municipality), 'cp_' (postal code), and 'refer_' (reference). The dataset is structured in two rows, with the first row containing the column names in Spanish and the second row providing the English translations."
