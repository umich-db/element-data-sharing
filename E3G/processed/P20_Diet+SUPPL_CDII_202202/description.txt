"This dataset comprises four columns: 'foliocc', 'cd_age', 'P20supp_cdii', and 'Etapa'. The 'foliocc' column serves as a unique identifier, 'cd_age' records the age of the child, 'P20supp_cdii' contains the P20 supplement child dietary inflammatory index score, and 'Etapa' indicates the stage. Each row in the dataset represents a unique record providing specific details about a child's age, dietary inflammation score, and the corresponding stage of the study or assessment."
