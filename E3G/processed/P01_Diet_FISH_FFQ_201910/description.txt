"This dataset provides detailed information on the frequency and portion sizes of various food items consumed over specific periods, such as the past 7 days and the past 3 months. It includes a wide range of food categories like dairy products, fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, seafood, legumes, cereals, and beverages, among others. Each food item is assessed for the number of days per week and the number of times per day it was consumed, with additional details on portion sizes using standardized measurements. The dataset also includes questions about typical consumption patterns and the inclusion of supplements, aiming to capture a comprehensive dietary intake profile."
