"This dataset contains detailed survey data related to dental and oral health, collected under the project code \"CIRV\" as of July 7, 2020. The dataset includes multiple columns such as 'proyecto', 'folio', 'foliocc', 'stage', 'id', 'f_dia', 'f_mes', 'f_anio', 'fechac19', 'edad', 'cod_ent', and various 'c19' prefixed columns that capture responses to specific questions about dental visits, reasons for visits, dental pain, and overall oral health status. Each entry captures the respondent's age, date of the interview, and specific responses to structured questions about their dental health experiences, frequency of dental issues, and their perceptions about their oral health. The data is structured to facilitate analysis of dental health trends and needs among the surveyed population."
