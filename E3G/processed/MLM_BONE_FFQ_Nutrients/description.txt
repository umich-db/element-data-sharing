"This dataset, titled \"MANUAL DE VARIABLES,\" is part of the R01 - MLM-BONE project and focuses on FFQ-Nutrimentos, which involves nutritional data collection. It includes a wide range of variables related to dietary intake, such as energy (in kilocalories and kilojoules), macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, fats), micronutrients (vitamins and minerals), and specific fatty acids, all measured in various units like grams, milligrams, and micrograms. The dataset is structured with identifiers for participants and stages, and it provides detailed nutritional information, including amino acids, cholesterol, and different types of fatty acids, making it a comprehensive resource for nutritional analysis."
