"This dataset is part of the R01-MLM-BONE project and involves blood pressure monitoring using the WatchBP Office ABI by Microlife. It includes variables such as systolic and diastolic blood pressure measurements for both arms, pulse pressure, heart rate, and interarm differences in blood pressure. The dataset also records participant information, measurement types, and remarks on observations, providing a comprehensive overview of blood pressure data for analysis. Each entry is identified by a unique project identifier and includes details on the measurement sequence and study date."
