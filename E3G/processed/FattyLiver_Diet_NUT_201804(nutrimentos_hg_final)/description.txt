"This dataset provides a comprehensive nutritional profile for various food items, detailing both macro and micronutrient content per 100g. It includes basic nutritional components such as proteins, fats, carbohydrates, and water, alongside a detailed breakdown of vitamins, minerals, and specific fatty acids. Additionally, the dataset covers information on food fortification, indicating whether foods are enriched with nutrients like calcium, iron, zinc, vitamin A, and vitamin C. The data also includes identifiers for the source of nutrient data, food group classifications, and whether the food is processed, making it a valuable resource for nutritional analysis and dietary planning."
