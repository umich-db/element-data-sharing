"This dataset, titled \"MANUAL DE VARIABLES,\" is part of the R01 - MLM-BONE project and utilizes the Inbody version LB120 for body composition analysis. It includes a comprehensive range of variables related to participants' physical and health metrics, such as height, weight, body fat mass, skeletal muscle mass, and various impedance measurements at different frequencies. The dataset also provides normal range limits for these metrics, along with demographic information like age, gender, and date of birth. Additionally, it contains control and target values for weight and body composition, as well as observations and notes for each participant."
