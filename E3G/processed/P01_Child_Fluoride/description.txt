"This dataset contains detailed nutritional information for various food items, focusing on both macronutrients and micronutrients. It includes data fields such as protein, fat, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals content per 100g, along with specific details on food fortification and the presence of substances like caffeine and phytosterols. The dataset also categorizes foods based on their processing level and includes identifiers for sources of data and food classification. Additionally, it provides information on fortified foods, indicating whether foods are enriched with nutrients like calcium, iron, zinc, vitamin A, and vitamin C, which is crucial for assessing nutritional quality and planning dietary interventions."
