"This dataset encompasses a comprehensive list of nutritional components found in various foods, detailing both macro and micronutrients. It includes information on vitamins, minerals, proteins, carbohydrates, and fats, each quantified per 100 grams of food. Additionally, the dataset provides details on food sources, preparation states (cooked, raw, or prepared), and specific identifiers for fortified foods and those used in nutritional studies. The dataset is structured to aid in the homologation of nutrient data, ensuring consistency and reliability in nutritional studies and food databases."
