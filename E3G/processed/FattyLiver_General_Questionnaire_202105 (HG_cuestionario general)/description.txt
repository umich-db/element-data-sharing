"This dataset contains a comprehensive collection of variables related to a health and lifestyle survey, capturing detailed information on participants' demographics, health status, lifestyle choices, and living conditions. Each variable, such as 'folio', 'etapa', 'id', and 'sexo', is meticulously described with concepts, values, and observations to ensure clarity in data interpretation. The dataset includes data on personal identifiers, date and time of the survey, marital status, living arrangements, occupation, health-related behaviors like smoking and drinking, household characteristics, and medical history. Additionally, it records physical measurements such as blood pressure, weight, height, and waist circumference, providing a holistic view of the participants' health and lifestyle profiles."
