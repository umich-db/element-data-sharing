"This dataset, titled \"F0-CovidCall_Physical Activity (IPAQ),\" contains detailed information on physical activities of participants over the past 7 days, focusing on various intensities such as vigorous, moderate, and walking activities. The data includes multiple variables such as the duration of activities in minutes and hours, the number of days activities were performed, and MET-minute/week calculations for different activity intensities. Additionally, it categorizes physical activity levels into low, moderate, and high based on the International Physical Activity Questionnaire (IPAQ) standards. The dataset also provides truncated versions of activity minutes and MET-minute/week calculations, ensuring a comprehensive analysis of physical activity patterns among participants."
