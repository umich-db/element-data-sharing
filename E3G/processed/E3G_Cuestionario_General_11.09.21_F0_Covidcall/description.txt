"This dataset contains detailed survey data collected for a project, focusing on various aspects of respondents' living conditions, health, and lifestyle. The data includes multiple columns representing different survey questions related to household characteristics, personal habits, health status, and socioeconomic factors. Each entry provides responses to questions such as changes in domicile due to sanitary contingencies, employment status, internet access, and more detailed inquiries about personal and family health, smoking and drinking habits, and dietary patterns. The dataset is structured to facilitate comprehensive analysis of factors affecting individual and community well-being during specific periods, such as the COVID-19 pandemic."
