"This dataset contains a variety of socio-economic variables related to households and individuals. Columns include 'proyecto', 'folio', 'foliocc', 'etapa', 'participante', 'stage', and 'amai_6x7', with detailed descriptions for each, such as the head of household's identity, education levels, and the number of rooms and bathrooms in the household. The dataset also includes variables for internet access, the number of people over 14 in the household, car ownership, and specific scores used to calculate the Socio-Economic Status (SES) according to the AMAI 6x7 methodology. The 'amai_6x7' column categorizes SES into seven levels from A/B (highest) to E (lowest)."
