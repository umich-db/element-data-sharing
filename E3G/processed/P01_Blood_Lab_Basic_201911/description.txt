"This dataset originates from the INPer Laboratory and is part of the Project Triad, with data collection starting on June 12, 2015. It primarily consists of blood count results including various parameters such as leukocytes, erythrocytes, hemoglobin, hematocrit, and platelets, among others. Each parameter is detailed with descriptions and some have remarks, providing a comprehensive overview of the blood analysis results. The dataset includes both percentage and absolute counts for different types of cells like neutrophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, eosinophils, and basophils, and also records comments related to the observations."
