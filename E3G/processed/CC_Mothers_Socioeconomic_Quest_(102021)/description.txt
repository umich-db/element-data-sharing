"This dataset encompasses a comprehensive collection of variables related to a specific project, identified by columns such as 'proyecto', 'folio', and 'foliocc'. It includes detailed demographic information about participants such as 'sex_ch', 'edad_ch' (age), and 'fechanac_ch' (date of birth). The dataset also tracks the date of visit ('fecha_visita'), attendance ('asistencia'), and a wide array of survey responses across multiple sections (e.g., 'sc5' to 'sc475_f'). Additionally, it includes socio-economic indicators ('NC_SOCIO'), providing a holistic view of the participants' socio-demographic profiles and their responses to various survey questions."
