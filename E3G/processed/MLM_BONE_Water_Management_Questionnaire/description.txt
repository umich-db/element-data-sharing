"This dataset is a comprehensive survey on water supply and management, focusing on various aspects of water access and usage in households. It includes variables such as the source and frequency of water supply, methods of water storage and treatment, and the primary sources of water for drinking, cooking, and hygiene. The dataset captures detailed responses about water availability, storage practices, and the role of household members in managing water resources. It is structured to provide insights into the challenges and practices related to water supply in different living environments."
