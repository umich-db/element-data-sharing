"This dataset encompasses a comprehensive collection of food frequency questionnaires (FFQ) tailored for children under five years old, capturing detailed dietary intake information across various food categories. Each row represents a specific food item, with columns detailing the frequency of consumption, food descriptions in both Spanish and English, and additional nutritional information. The dataset includes over 100 columns, covering aspects from basic food identification and consumption frequency to detailed micronutrient and macronutrient data. This structured format allows for in-depth analysis of dietary patterns and nutritional intake, facilitating research in nutritional epidemiology and public health."
