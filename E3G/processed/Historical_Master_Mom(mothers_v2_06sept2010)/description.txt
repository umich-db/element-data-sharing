"This dataset encompasses a comprehensive collection of variables related to maternal and child health, capturing details from pregnancy through childbirth and postpartum periods. It includes demographic information, health status, environmental exposures, and lifestyle factors of both mothers and their children. Key data points include birth metrics, breastfeeding practices, nutritional habits, and medical history, alongside detailed records on environmental factors such as water source and traffic conditions. The dataset is structured to facilitate analysis on the impact of various factors on health outcomes, making it a valuable resource for researchers studying maternal and child health."
