"This dataset comprises a comprehensive collection of variables related to child development, environmental factors, and nutritional data. It includes detailed information on project identifiers, demographic data, environmental exposures, dietary intakes, psychological assessments, and health-related measurements. Each variable is meticulously documented with attributes such as type, length, format, and descriptive labels to ensure clarity and ease of use. The dataset is structured to facilitate in-depth analysis of factors influencing child development, making it a valuable resource for researchers in the fields of pediatrics, psychology, and environmental health."
