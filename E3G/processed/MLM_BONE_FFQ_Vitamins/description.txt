"This dataset, titled \"MANUAL DE VARIABLES,\" is part of the R01 - MLM-BONE project and focuses on the FFQ-Vitaminas study. It contains a comprehensive list of variables related to dietary intake, including vitamins, minerals, and other nutritional components, measured in various units such as micrograms, milligrams, and international units per day. The dataset includes participant information, study stages, and identifiers, providing a detailed overview of nutritional intake and its components for the study participants. Key variables include vitamins A, B, C, D, E, and K, as well as minerals like calcium, iron, magnesium, and zinc, among others."
