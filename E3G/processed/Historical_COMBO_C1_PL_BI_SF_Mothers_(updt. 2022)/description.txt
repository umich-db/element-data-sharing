"This dataset encompasses a comprehensive collection of variables related to maternal and child health, capturing data across various stages of pregnancy and postpartum periods. It includes identifiers, sociodemographic details, health status, lifestyle factors, and environmental exposures. The data fields cover a wide range of topics such as hospital details, type of participant, educational background, household characteristics, employment, use of ceramics, water consumption, smoking habits, breastfeeding practices, medication usage, baby's food, health issues of the baby and mother, reproductive health of the mother, anthropometry, blood pressure, physical activity, supplementation, sample collection, and family allergies. Each variable is meticulously coded to facilitate detailed analysis, aiming to provide insights into health outcomes and influences during critical developmental periods."
