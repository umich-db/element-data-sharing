"This dataset contains detailed anthropometric measurements and blood pressure data collected from a cohort study, as outlined in the \"Anthropometry and Blood Pressure Manual\". The dataset includes variables such as cohort ID, visit name, measurement dates, gender, age in days, months, and years, weight, height, BMI, waist circumference, and various z-scores (WAZ, HAZ, BAZ). Additionally, it captures data on arm measurements, skin folds, systolic and diastolic blood pressures, and whether specific studies like Inbody, CPT, and NIH Toolbox were performed. Flags are used to indicate outliers or data range issues based on predefined criteria."
