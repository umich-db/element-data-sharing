"This dataset provides a comprehensive nutritional profile for various food items, detailing both macro and micronutrient content per 100g. It includes information on proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals, and other specific nutrients like caffeine and theobromine. Additionally, the dataset identifies whether foods are fortified with nutrients such as calcium, iron, zinc, vitamin A, and vitamin C, and provides details on the source of nutrient data, whether the food is processed, and the form in which the food is typically consumed (cooked, raw, or prepared). This dataset is a valuable resource for nutritional analysis, diet planning, and public health research, offering insights into the nutritional composition of a wide range of food items."
