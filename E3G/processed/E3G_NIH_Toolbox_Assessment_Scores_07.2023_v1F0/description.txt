This dataset contains a wide array of variables related to participant assessments and device usage within a specific project. Key variables include project identifiers, participant folios, stages of data collection, and assessment details. The dataset tracks various participant information such as the date and location of assessments, device IDs, and PINs. It also includes comprehensive assessment data, capturing raw scores, theta values, T-scores, standard errors, item counts, and dates of completion. Additionally, the dataset records technical details about the devices used, such as app version, iPad version, and firmware version. The structure of the dataset is designed to provide a complete overview of the assessment process, from participant information to technical specifications.
