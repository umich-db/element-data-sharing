"This dataset contains various measurements related to fluoride consumption and its sources. The columns include 'FOLIOCC' as an ID, 'etapa' indicating the visit stage, 'peso' representing weight in kg, and several columns detailing fluoride intake from different sources and calculations: 'f_usda' shows fluoride consumption using USDA tables, 'fluoruros' using market basket values, and 'fluoruros_beb' specifically from beverages like tea, coffee, and sodas. Additional metrics include 'porc_fluor_bebidas' which is the percentage of fluoride intake from beverages, and 'fluor_peso' representing fluoride consumption relative to body weight."
